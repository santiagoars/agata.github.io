About Us

Agata´s main goal is to show mexican jewelry, art and culture to the world. This idea started when we thought of Mexico as a country with a lot of diversity, culture, traditions and colors. And that’s the reason why we created Agata, as a way to give an opportunity for everyone to look, get to know, and buy pieces of Mexican artistic-jewelry, or as we like to say, pieces of Mexican culture and traditions. We are partnered with a handcrafted jewelry sculptor who works in Tlaquepaque, Jalisco. And we are aiming to work with more sculptors in the future. One of the fundamental values of Agata is to always respect and give value to the art pieces they make. As we said, all the jewelry we sell is hand crafted and some of these pieces are unique in the world. That’s because each of the patterns of the quartz, amethyst, etc. cannot be the same. In that way, every piece you buy will have a special value, because you will be the only one in the world to have that piece. So we hope you give us the opportunity to show you a little bit of Mexico’s art, jewelry and culture. And also the opportunity of making you happy by having one or more pieces of Agata´s products.


As you know recently an earthquake took place in Mexico City, and many people needs help. That’s why Agata will donate 75% of the profits to those persons in need.
